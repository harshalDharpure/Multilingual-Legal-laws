## Journal Revision Package Checklist (KBS)

Files (DOCX unless noted):
- Cover letter (revised)
- Response to reviewers (point-by-point rebuttal table)
- Highlights (3–5 bullet points)
- Revised manuscript (tracked changes)
- Clean manuscript (no track changes)
- CRediT author statement
- Author agreement
- Declaration of interest
- Editable figures (300 dpi; not PDF) and tables
- Source (LaTeX or Word) + .bib/.cls if LaTeX
- Optional: MATLAB .FIG for interactive figures; AudioSlides later

Artifacts from this repo to cite/attach:
- CALSD-Real subset specs and files
- Human Eval Protocol and anonymized rating form
- Robustness/OOD evaluation plan + results tables/figures
- Pipeline simplification (gating) results
- CoV fallacy detection prompt/spec and examples
- Data split policy, leakage controls, and OOD results

Submission system:
- Upload via Editorial Manager → Submissions Needing Revision
- Ensure all references/tables/figures are cited in-text
